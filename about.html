Our Story

AquaShine was founded with a simple mission: to revolutionize the car wash industry with technology that's both effective and environmentally responsible.

What started as a single location has grown into a network of premium car wash facilities, each equipped with the latest touchless cleaning technology and staffed by trained professionals who share our passion for excellence.

Today, we serve thousands of customers weekly, from everyday commuters to luxury car enthusiasts, all trusting us to deliver the perfect shine every single time.
